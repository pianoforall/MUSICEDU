About Piano for ALL

Modern, friendly piano and music theory lessons aligned with ABRSM & Trinity, offered online (Zoom/Google Meet) and in person.

Our Mission

We help students of all ages become confident, musical, and exam-ready pianists. Lessons combine strong foundations (technique & theory) with creativity and performance skills.

About the Teacher

  • ABRSM/Trinity trained with experience teaching Grades 1–8.
  • Prepares students for **both** practical and theory exams.
  • Patient, structured approach tailored to each learner.
  • Fluent with online learning tools (Zoom, Google Meet, digital scores).

Teaching Approach

  • Technique first: posture, hand shape, tone, pedal.
  • Musicianship: sight-reading, aural, rhythm & expression.
  • Theory integrated: harmony, scales, and analysis connected to repertoire.
  • Practice plans: weekly goals and feedback to keep progress steady.

What You’ll Need

  • Acoustic piano or 61+ key touch-sensitive keyboard.
  • Stable internet, camera, and microphone for online lessons.
  • ABRSM/Trinity books (we’ll advise exact editions per grade).

Student Results & Highlights

  • Consistent passes in ABRSM theory and practical exams (Grades 1–8).
  • Beginner students performing full pieces within the first months.
  • Regular mock exams and online recitals to build confidence.

Platforms

Online lessons via Zoom or Google Meet. In-person lessons available for home tutoring.

FAQs

What ages do you teach?

Children (6+) to adults. Lessons are adapted to each learner’s pace and goals.

Do I need prior experience?

No. We welcome complete beginners to advanced students preparing for exams.

How do payments work?

Fees are listed on the Pricing page and paid per 4-week block (or per course).
